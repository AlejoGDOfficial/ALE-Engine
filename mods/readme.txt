=== Mod Installation Guide ===

✅ Correct Structure (Required): 
mods/
└── Mod Name/  
    ├── images/
    ├── songs/
    ├── data/
    └── ...

❌ Incorrect Structure (Not Supported):
mods/  
├── images/  
├── songs/  
├── data/  
└── ...

⚠️ Important: Mods created for Psych Engine are NOT fully supported.
🔧 Adjustments may be required to ensure compatibility.

🎮 Follow these guidelines to avoid issues.
